Nigerian Jellof Rice

Spices and stewed in a flavourful tomatoe sauce, jellof rice is a beloved west african dish. Its preparatory is decribed below

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Vegetable broth
  • Tomatoes(fresh and pureed)
  • Onion, oil, pepper, bay leaves
  • Salt and seasoning cube

Steps:

  1. Wash rice and set aside
  2. Saute your tomatoes, onions and pepper in a heated oil
  3. Pour the vegetable broth into the tomatoe sauce
  4. Add salt, seasoning cubes and bay leaves
  5. When the broth has boiled, pourin the washed rice and cover the pot.
  6. Food is ready when the liquid is all dried up
