Homemade Lasagna

A delicious homemade lasagna with beef and rich creamy cheese topping, a classic comfort food.

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Bell peppers, onions and garlic
  • Tomatoe sauce
  • Cheese(shredded mozzarella and parmesan)
  • Flat noodles

Steps:

  1. Make the sauce by sauteing the tomatoe sauce,bell peppers, onion, garlic and ground beef.
  2. While the tomatoe sauce is simmering, boil the noodles and get the cheese ready.
  3. Afterwards, assemble in a lasagna bowl by adding a cup of meat sauce, a layer of noodles and more sauce, then top with cheese. repeat until all ingredients are used.
  4. Bake until it's done and ready to eat.
