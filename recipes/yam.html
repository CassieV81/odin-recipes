Yam Porridge

Delicious Porridge Yam

Yam porridge is a delicious Nigerian dish, it is quickand easy to make and it can be enjoyed as a lunch or a dinner.

Ingredients

  • Yam
  • Red oil
  • Spinach
  • Onions and pepper
  • Salt and seasoning cube

Steps

  1. Peel the yam, cut into big cubes, wash and putinto a pot.
  2. Add water to the same level as the yam, add salt, seasoning cube, red oil,onions and pepper. then cover pot
  3. WHen the yam has softened, add spinach and cover pot again and wait
  4. After a few minutes,food is ready to serve
